Building large language models from the ground up using PyTorch. Implements character-level bigram models, scaled dot-product attention, multi-head attention, and transformer blocks following the GPT architecture. Covers the full pipeline from tokenization to training, including GPU optimization with CUDA, learning rate scheduling, and text generation with temperature sampling.
