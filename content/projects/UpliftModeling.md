Uplift modeling project for the RetailHero competition, ranking customers by the effectiveness of marketing communication. Implements multiple uplift modeling approaches including S-learner, T-learner, and X-learner meta-learners using EconML. Processes customer profiles, purchase history, and campaign response data to identify persuadable customers who are most likely to respond positively to targeted interventions.
